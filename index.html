    Trust and Commitment

    About Volunteers for the Blind

    We are a voluntary organization working in the field of rehabilitation of the blind. We work with NGO’s & with Visually Challenged individuals. We network with them offering support and assistance on a national basis ensuring that quality inputs are provided which are the best in the country. We execute our programmes through Volunteers. Our activities include projects and programmes of our choice, priorities desired by beneficiary NGOs, orientation and motivation of volunteers, sharing of information, assistance to government & development of technology applications. The organization has Members & Volunteers. These are spread out all over the country.

    HOW IS VFTB GOVERNED

    The Trustees
    The Trustees make all strategic & financial decisions, has overall responsibility for all VB activities.
    The Forum
    The Forum, is accountable to the Trustees; decides policy & rules; holds the board accountable for its governance activities.
    The Board of Trustees
    The Board is directly accountable to the Forum & indirectly to the Trustees; carries out all executive actions & supervises the work at each city. The Chairperson reports to the Trustees.
    Governing Councils
    The Councils are directly accountable to the Board; carry out all executive actions, training & supervise the work at each city.

    WHAT MAKES US UNIQUE

    1. We have voluntarily introduced democratic principles within the organisation, though the law does not expect the same. We conduct annual elections for the Board of Directors as well as for city level Governing Councils.
    2. The inclusion of the blind in the decision making process, is a conscious effort.
    3. With 20 million blind persons in India, the numbers are huge & numerous organisations like ours, doing networking, will fall short of the need. However, we are the only organisation of its kind, in India, currently working with this concept & approach. All others are a school, a vocational centre, a hostel or a combination of these, providing services for blind individuals.
    4. We bring the best resources to bear, not merely what we our members can put together. We approach organisations & individuals who may be needed, not limiting ourselves to our members or partner Institutions.
    5. We’ve learnt the methods & supervisory techniques for massed deployment of volunteers. We know how to manage & effectively use hundreds of volunteers, simultaneously, on a single project.

    OUR VALUES

    Empowerment of the Blind.
    By constitution, we give greater control to the Blind. A minimum of 33% of members need to be Visually Challenged. We encourage greater numbers to join. They are included in all Office bearer levels & subcommittees & are consulted on every issue. Their views carry greater weightage. All issues important to the Blind are important to us. We respond wherever an opportunity to benefit the Blind, presents itself. We cooperate in multi disability matters, where blindness is the common refrain. We do not work in areas where the Blind are not involved. But, we are willing to work in cross disability areas where benefits will accrue to the Blind as well. We cater for all age groups, social backgrounds, gender & all spheres of activity affecting the blind.
    Institutional & Individual Support
    We network with all NGOs; some are larger & more efficient, thereby bringing the benefits of the strengths of one to the other. So, if an NGO wishes to grow bigger or refine their existing services, we assist. We network with Resource Persons, volunteers & those sympathetic to the cause of the blind, thereby bringing the best skills & resources on the cutting edge. We directly provide services for individual blind persons. The needs of such individuals are also met through our partner NGOs.
    Impact all Indian Blind Persons.
    We offer services for the Indian Blind, at City & National levels, partnering with Volunteers, Donors & NGOs across the country & the world. The Indian Blind don’t live only in India. We aspire to reach out overseas as well. Our planning processes are National & in consonance with those of the largest NGOs, with Government & with MultiNational Corporations.
    Satisfaction of the Blind
    No refusals. All Donors, Volunteers and NGOs are accepted, except those with conditional offers subject to biases in age, gender, religion, race, caste or political influence. We respond to all requests, and suitable records are maintained. Quick and rapid response and provision of professional services are undertaken. Volunteer teams are held in readiness. Volunteers are well trained, motivated, provided suitable incentives & screened to check biases. We encourage professionalism & quality services. Priorities of VH individuals or NGOs are priorities for us. We stay updated and informed of all activities in the Disability sector. We get involved and are active with the leading organisations in the sector.
